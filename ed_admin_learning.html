Ed-admin Learning

A Complete Package for Your Institution

Powerful and intuitive learning management software

Ed-admin Learning is a browser-based application that streamlines and simplifies education management for institutions at all levels. It integrates administrative, teaching, and learning tasks into one user-friendly, custom-designed database.

Designed and developed by a core team of scholastic specialists and software developers, Ed-admin Learning provides a versatile system that grows with your needs and integrates seamlessly into any environment — from specialised academia to large corporations.

Learn More

Manage finances with our time-saving tools

Keep all the necessary tools to manage your finances in one place. From customer records and staff payroll to tuition and application fees. Use batch payments, automatic statements, and payment gateways to save time and keep parents, customers, and suppliers satisfied.

Manage the admission process from enquiry to enrollments

Connect our Admission module to your website and enjoy automated processes from enquiry to admission, re-enrollment, and online fee collection.

Grow your academy with analytics

Identify strengths and weaknesses with advanced analytics. Assessment, behavioural observation, activity management, generated report cards and more — all in one place.

Assessment Premium

A comprehensive online grade book that incorporates various formats to create and print any report card or transcript. Learning and Behaviour Management

Full Learning and Behaviour management system, with optional automated processes that allow for students to be rewarded or penalised for their actions, on a merit/demerit system. Includes a multi-level security access control system to dictate who can and can’t access the records, either through the mobile apps or web portal for both parents and staff. Automated notifications and workflow management of student behavioural incidents are also catered for.

Learn More
Curriculum Management

Easily standardise your curriculum with these customisable curriculum management tools, adhering to national competency standards and tracking changes over the years. Integrated Library Management System

Integrated library system with ISBN auto-cataloguing, barcode scan functionality, and a library search feature. Easily manage all books in and out of the system, as well as an in-depth overdue system linked to the finance modules. A public-facing portal allows parents and students to search for books, and a comprehensive in-app interface enables tracking of current and previous books as well as a return reminder for students and parents.

Learning Support
