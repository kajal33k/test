IFNOSS UCMS - University and College Management Software

IFNOSS UCMS is a complete education ERP software solution for universities and colleges to streamline and automate their activities. This university management system software is designed specifically for all kinds of small and large universities and colleges. IFNOSS supports universities to build, manage, and extend their digital campus.

Specifically Built for Higher Education

our ERP systems enable you to:

Automate admissions

Eliminate manual processes and save significant staff time by enabling prospective students to apply online through a self-service portal

Provide one-stop student access

Allow students to enroll, register, and pay for courses through the portal

Simplify records management

With a single system for all your data needs—and a single digital record for each student—any department on campus can find the student information they need

Engage faculty

Give faculty the means to enter and update grades, and have personalized access to timely, accurate, and institution-wide information

Manage resources

Manage personnel, funds and processes more transparently

Strengthen decision-making

Track the metrics you need to guide day-to-day operations, meet reporting requirements, and engage your organization

Integration with QuickBooks

IFNOSS software for universities and colleages integrates with quickbooks for accounting purpose. All the entries done in the software with regards to invoices, receipts, payments and estimates (proforma invoices) can be pushed into quickbooks automatically while saving the data.
